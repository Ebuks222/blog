**Enugu Police Arrest 20 Suspected Cultists, Recover Hard Drugs, 22 Motorcycles**



**The Enugu State Police Command has arrested 20 suspected cultists and recovered hard drugs, 22 motorcycles, a tricycle, an axe, and other incriminating exhibits during intelligence-led operations carried out across the state between July 7 and 14, 2026.**

**The operations, conducted by operatives of the Command's Violent Crime Response Unit (VCRU), targeted criminal hideouts and suspected cult groups involved in robbery, drug trafficking, and other violent crimes.**

**Police arrested two suspects during a raid at Banana Estate, Independence Layout, where suspected cultists were allegedly terrorising residents, while six others were apprehended in separate raids on criminal hideouts in Emene and Eke Obinagu, with suspected cannabis, methamphetamine, and cocaine recovered.**

**In another operation, officers disrupted the alleged 7/7 anniversary celebration of the Black Axe (Aye) Confraternity in Udenu Local Government Area, arresting 12 suspects and recovering an axe, drum instruments, and 14 motorcycles.**

**Commissioner of Police, CP Mamman Bitrus Giwa, commended the operatives for the**

**successful operations, reaffirming the Command's commitment to combating cultism, drug trafficking, and other violent crimes. He urged residents to continue supporting the police with timely and credible information.**
